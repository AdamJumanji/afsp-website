Food Product Development

Product development programs requiring processing or manufacturing can be costly and time-consuming; many good products have failed to reach the market because of delays in development and production, over-expended budgets, poor timing or missed opportunities. We are pleased to introduce to you very cost-effective, highly skilled professional and confidential services of ALMAAS Food Safety Plus for the food, cosmetic and household products. Our formula for success is to bring technical expertise and management skills together into a uniquely efficient service-oriented combination to truly respond to the needs of our clients in the following areas:

  • Projects from Concept to the Reality of Production Implementation
  • Product Formulation/Reformulation
  • Cost Reduction Controls
  • Product/Process Shelf-Life Improvements
  • Feasibility Studies
  • Flavor and Fragrance Development

ALMAAS brings together the multi-disciplinary resources of its highly motivated personnel who combine their creative ideas, strong technical background and extensive experience in the product and process development. This effective combination of diverse disciplines, augmented by the staff and client interaction, results in a methodical and practical approach to problem solving leading to innovative, viable, cost-effective solutions to product and process development.

We have worked in Canada and the overseas market developing products and processes in a Canadian research environment; and have formulated and re-formulated hundreds of food, cosmetic and household products to improve processing, products, reduce costs or improve profitability.
