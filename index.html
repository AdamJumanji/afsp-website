About Us

ALMAAS Food Safety Plus (AFSP) is a leading Quality Management and Food Safety Consulting company based in North America with extensive operations in the Middle East and North Africa (MENA). Our commitment of professionalism and confidentiality to our customers is evident in everything we have accomplished. We align ourselves with our customers as partners in assisting them to improve their competitiveness, profitability and to achieve their goals and objectives.

Vision

ALMAAS is striving to make quality management, food safety training and certification a global priority, an organizational imperative culture and a personal ethical commitment.

Mission

A passionate advocate for food safety aims to improve and increase competence and skills in safe food handling and to mandate compulsory certification for every person working in the food industry. To provide innovative and creative solutions to challenges faced by the food industry.

Food Safety

The ALMAAS team of professional trainers are certified and registered in Canada. They have the academic food science expertise coupled with extensive experience in the food industry and the knowledge in helping business owners and managers develop and sustain a successful food safety culture in the work place that serves to protect both consumers and workers.

In-Class Training

One-day, on-site, food safety certification courses for food service and retail workers and management. Taught in English or Arabic, all courses are based on Canadian Food Retail and Food Services Regulations and Code, Health Canada and the Canadian Food Inspection Agency (CFIA).
